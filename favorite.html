HOME

HOME is an animated comedy film about an alien named Oh, who is part of an alien species called the Boov. After an invasion of Earth, Oh befriends a young girl named Tip, and together they embark on an adventure to fix their mistakes and save the planet. It's a heartwarming tale of friendship and belonging.

Main Characters

Oh: Oh is a friendly and clumsy Boov alien. His kind tries to conquer Earth, but after a series of events, he becomes an unlikely hero. Despite his awkwardness, Oh is kind-hearted and loyal, learning the importance of friendship throughout the film.

Tip: Tip is a smart and courageous young girl who becomes Oh’s best friend. After being separated from her mother during the Boov invasion, Tip teams up with Oh to find her, showing determination and bravery despite the challenges she faces.

The Plot

The plot of Home revolves around Oh's accidental mischief leading to the discovery of Earth’s true value. After being blamed for a disastrous mistake, Oh escapes from his fellow Boov, encountering Tip. Together, they travel across the world to rescue Tip’s mother and stop the Boov's invasion from being a complete failure.

The Setting

The movie is set on Earth, which has been taken over by the Boov, a race of aliens that are trying to relocate the human population. The setting shifts from cityscapes to more intimate, emotional places as Tip and Oh’s journey unfolds, emphasizing the contrast between alien technology and human nature.

Personal Thoughts

I love the themes of friendship and self-identity in Home. The relationship between Tip and Oh teaches us about understanding others' differences and finding strength in companionship. It's a fun, emotional ride with great humor and an important message about kindness and home.
